1Password's Watchtower monitors your stored passwords against known breaches in real-time. Travel Mode lets you hide specific vaults when crossing borders ‚Äî useful if you're concerned about device searches at customs. The secret key system (a second factor required to decrypt your vault) adds meaningful protection against server breaches.

Bitwarden's open-source code has been independently audited by Cure53 (2020, 2022). Self-hosting is possible for users who want complete control over their vault data. The free tier genuinely stores unlimited passwords across unlimited devices ‚Äî there's no artificial cap forcing you to upgrade.

Recommendation

Choose Bitwarden if: budget matters, you prefer open-source software, or you want to self-host. Choose 1Password if: you want the most polished UX, need Travel Mode, or value 24/7 customer support.

Both are dramatically better than reusing passwords or relying on browser-saved passwords (which are readable by malware with local access).

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Bitwarden really free forever? A: The free tier is truly free with unlimited passwords and devices. The $10/year Premium adds TOTP authenticator integration and priority support.

Q: What is 1Password Travel Mode? A: You can hide specific vaults when crossing borders. Customs officials who inspect your device only see the vaults you've left visible. Hidden vaults are inaccessible even if the device is unlocked.

Q: Can I switch from 1Password to Bitwarden? A: Yes. 1Password exports to CSV or 1PUX format. Bitwarden imports 1Password exports directly. The migration takes about 5 minutes.

Q: What encryption does Bitwarden use? A: AES-256-CBC with PBKDF2 SHA-256 key derivation. Comparable to 1Password. Both are industry-standard and not practically breakable.
